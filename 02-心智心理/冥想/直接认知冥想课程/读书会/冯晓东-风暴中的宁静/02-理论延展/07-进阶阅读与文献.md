## 二、正念/MBCT/CBT 经典文献 / Classic Literature

### MBSR（正念减压疗法）

> 卡巴金（Jon Kabat-Zinn）创立的正念减压项目，是现代正念运动的源头。

- Kabat-Zinn, J. (1990). *Full Catastrophe Living: Using the Wisdom of Your Body and Mind to Face Stress, Pain, and Illness*. New York: Delta. **[MBSR创始著作 / Foundational text]**
  - 中文版：卡巴金 著, 王俊兰 译.《多舛的生命：运用身体和心灵的智慧面对压力、疼痛和疾病》(原书名 *Full Catastrophe Living*). 机械工业出版社, 2018. · 微信读书版书名:《多舛的生命:正念疗愈帮你抚平压力、疼痛和创伤(原书第2版)》
- Kabat-Zinn, J. (1994). *Wherever You Go, There You Are: Mindfulness Meditation in Everyday Life*. New York: Hyperion.
  - 中文版：卡巴金 著, 王俊兰 译.《正念：此刻是一枝花》. 机械工业出版社, 2015.
- Kabat-Zinn, J. (2005). *Coming to Our Senses: Healing Ourselves and the World Through Mindfulness*. New York: Hyperion.
- Santorelli, S. F. (2014). *Mindfulness-Based Stress Reduction (MBSR): Standards of Practice*. Center for Mindfulness, UMass Medical School. **[MBSR标准操作手册 / Standards of practice]**

### MBCT（正念认知疗法）

> 本书第 4 章「正念认知」的核心理论参照。MBCT 由三位创始人开发，专门用于抑郁复发预防，后来扩展到焦虑。

- Segal, Z. V., Williams, J. M. G., & Teasdale, J. D. (2002/2013). *Mindfulness-Based Cognitive Therapy for Depression*. New York: Guilford Press. **[MBCT创始著作 / Foundational text]**
  - 中文版：西格尔、威廉姆斯、蒂斯代尔 著.《抑郁症的内观认知疗法》. 世界图书出版公司, 2017.
- Williams, M., Teasdale, J., Segal, Z., & Kabat-Zinn, J. (2007). *The Mindful Way through Depression: Freeing Yourself from Chronic Unhappiness*. New York: Guilford Press. **[面向大众的MBCT入门 / Popular MBCT introduction]**
  - 中文版：威廉姆斯 等 著, 谭浩清 译.《改善情绪的正念疗法》. 中国人民大学出版社, 2015.
- Kuyken, W., et al. (2016). "Efficacy of Mindfulness-Based Cognitive Therapy in Prevention of Depressive Relapse: An Individual Patient Data Meta-analysis from Randomized Trials." *JAMA Psychiatry* 73(6): 565–574. **[MBCT元分析循证 / Meta-analytic evidence]**

### CBT（认知行为疗法）

> 本书第 2、4 章的认知框架来源。

- Beck, A. T. (1976). *Cognitive Therapy and the Emotional Disorders*. New York: International Universities Press. **[认知疗法创始著作 / Foundational text]**
- Beck, J. S. (2011). *Cognitive Behavior Therapy: Basics and Beyond*. 2nd ed. New York: Guilford Press. **[CBT标准教材 / Standard textbook]**
  - 中文版：贝克 著, 王建平等 译.《认知疗法：基础与进阶》. 中国轻工业出版社, 2013.
- Burns, D. D. (1980). *Feeling Good: The New Mood Therapy*. New York: Signet. **[面向大众的CBT经典 / Popular CBT classic]**
  - 中文版：伯恩斯 著, 李亚萍 译.《伯恩斯新情绪疗法》. 中国城市出版社, 2011.
- Clark, D. A. & Beck, A. T. (2010). *Cognitive Therapy of Anxiety Disorders: Science and Practice*. New York: Guilford Press. **[焦虑CBT权威 / Authoritative CBT for anxiety]**

### CBT-I（失眠认知行为疗法）

> 本书助眠章的参照系。

- Edinger, J. D. & Carney, C. E. (2014). *Overcoming Insomnia: A Cognitive-Behavioral Therapy Approach, Therapist Guide*. 2nd ed. Oxford: Oxford University Press.
- Morin, C. I. & Espie, C. A. (2012). *Insomnia: A Clinical Guide to Assessment and Treatment*. New York: Springer. **[失眠临床指南 / Clinical guide]**
- Ong, J. C., et al. (2014). "A Mindfulness-Based Approach to the Treatment of Insomnia." In *Sleep and Psychosomatic Medicine*, 2nd ed. **[正念助眠研究 / Mindfulness for insomnia research]**

### ACT（接纳承诺疗法）

> 与本书"接纳"概念高度平行，虽非本书直接参照，但理论上有深层关联。

- Hayes, S. C., Strosahl, K. D., & Wilson, K. G. (1999/2012). *Acceptance and Commitment Therapy: The Process and Practice of Mindful Change*. 2nd ed. New York: Guilford Press. **[ACT创始著作 / Foundational text]**
  - 中文版：海斯 等 著, 祝卓宏 等译.《接纳承诺疗法：正念改变之道》. 知识产权出版社, 2017.
- Harris, R. (2008). *The Happiness Trap: How to Stop Struggling and Start Living*. Wollombi: Exisle. **[面向大众的ACT入门 / Popular ACT introduction]**

## 三、佛教心理学与正念传统 / Buddhist Psychology & Mindfulness Tradition

### 佛教心理学基础

- Analayo, B. (2003). *Satipaṭṭhāna: The Direct Path to Realization*. Birmingham: Windhorse Publications. **[四念处研究权威 / Authoritative study on Satipatthana]**
  - 中文版：无着比丘 著.《念处之道：大念处经解析》. 宗教文化出版社, 2018.
- Goleman, D. (2003). *Destructive Emotions: A Scientific Dialogue with the Dalai Lama*. New York: Bantam. **[佛教心理学与神经科学对话 / Buddhism-neuroscience dialogue]**
  - 中文版：戈尔曼 著.《破坏性情绪》. 北京：中国长安出版社, 2006.
- Wallace, B. A. (2007). *Contemplative Science: Where Buddhism and Neuroscience Converge*. New York: Columbia University Press. **[禅修科学研究 / Contemplative science]**

### 五蕴与唯识学

> 本书第 2 章五蕴模型的源头。

- 菩提比丘（Bhikkhu Bodhi）编.《阿毗达磨概要精解》. 宗教文化出版社, 2011. **[南传阿毗达磨中的五蕴分析 / Abhidhamma analysis of five aggregates]**
- 玄奘 译.《成唯识论》. 收入《大正藏》第31册. **[唯识学根本论典 / Foundational Yogācāra text]**
- 印顺法师.《唯识学探源》. 妙林出版社, 1992. **[中文唯识研究经典 / Classic Chinese Yogācāra study]**

### 内观（Vipassanā）传统

- 葛印卡（S. N. Goenka）.《内观禅修入门》. 海南出版社, 2014. **[内观实修入门 / Vipassana practice introduction]**
- Goldstein, J. (2013). *Mindfulness: A Practical Guide to Awakening*. Louisville, CO: Sounds True. **[内观现代阐述 / Modern Vipassana exposition]**

## 四、神经科学与正念研究 / Neuroscience & Mindfulness Research

### 正念的神经科学

- Davidson, R. J., et al. (2003). "Alterations in Brain and Immune Function Produced by Mindfulness Meditation." *Psychosomatic Medicine* 65(4): 564–570. **[MBSR神经免疫学开创性研究 / Landmark MBSR neuroimmunology study]**
- Lazar, S. W., et al. (2005). "Meditation Experience is Associated with Increased Cortical Thickness." *NeuroReport* 16(17): 1893–1897. **[正念改变大脑结构 / Meditation changes brain structure]**
- Tang, Y. Y., Hölzel, B. K., & Posner, M. I. (2015). "The Neuroscience of Mindfulness Meditation." *Nature Reviews Neuroscience* 16(4): 213–225. **[正念神经科学综述 / Comprehensive review]**
  - 中文综述：唐一源 等. 正念冥想的神经科学. 收入《心理学报》相关专题.

### 正念与情绪调节

- Chiesa, A. & Serretti, A. (2009). "Mindfulness-Based Stress Reduction for Stress Management in Healthy People: A Review and Meta-analysis." *Journal of Alternative and Complementary Medicine* 15(5): 593–600.
- Hofmann, S. G., et al. (2010). "The Effect of Mindfulness-Based Therapy on Anxiety and Mood: A Meta-analytic Review." *Journal of Consulting and Clinical Psychology* 78(2): 169–183. **[正念对焦虑/抑郁效果的元分析 / Meta-analysis on mindfulness for anxiety/depression]**

### 正念与睡眠

- Black, D. S., et al. (2015). "Mindfulness Meditation and Improvement in Sleep Quality and Daytime Impairment Among Older Adults with Sleep Disturbances." *JAMA Internal Medicine* 175(4): 494–501. **[正念改善睡眠的随机对照试验 / RCT on mindfulness for sleep]**
- Ong, J. C., et al. (2014). "A Mindfulness-Based Approach to the Treatment of Insomnia." In *Sleep and Psychosomatic Medicine*.
