## 一、流行病学

- **患病率**:全球 1-3%(世界卫生组织)
- **共病**:抑郁(60-80%)、焦虑(50%)、抽动障碍(30%)
- **病程**:慢性波动性,常起病于青少年期
- **参考**:American Psychiatric Association. (2013). *DSM-5*. https://doi.org/10.1176/appi.books.9780890425596

## 二、循证治疗

### 2.1 一线治疗:ERP(暴露与反应预防)

#### 机制
- **暴露(Exposure)**:主动面对引发强迫思维的情境
- **反应预防(Response Prevention)**:阻止执行强迫行为
- 通过习惯化 + 抑制学习降低焦虑

#### 疗效
- 50-60% 患者显著改善(YBOCS 减分 ≥35%)
- 长期效果优于药物治疗
- **参考**:
  - Foa, E. B., & Kozak, M. J. (1996). Psychological treatment for obsessive-compulsive disorder. In M. R. Mavissakalian & R. F. Prien (Eds.), *Long-term treatments of anxiety disorders* (pp. 285-309). American Psychiatric Press.
  - Franklin, M. E., & Foa, E. B. (2011). Treatment of obsessive compulsive disorder. *Annual Review of Clinical Psychology*, *7*, 229-243. https://doi.org/10.1146/annurev-clinpsy-032210-104533

#### 实施
- 12-20 次治疗(每周 1-2 次)
- 90 分钟/次
- 家庭参与(尤其儿童青少年)

### 2.2 二线治疗:CBT 增强

- 认知重构(Cognitive Restructuring)
- 思维记录表
- **参考**:Wilhelm, S., & Steketee, G. S. (2006). *Cognitive therapy for obsessive-compulsive disorder: A guide for professionals*. New Harbinger.

### 2.3 药物治疗

#### 一线:SSRI
- **药物**:氟西汀、舍曲林、氟伏沙明、帕罗西汀
- **剂量**:通常为治疗抑郁的 2-3 倍
- **起效时间**:8-12 周(非 2-4 周)
- **参考**:
  - Fineberg, N. A., et al. (2012). Evidence-based pharmacotherapy of obsessive-compulsive disorder. *International Journal of Neuropsychopharmacology*, *15*(8), 1173-1191. https://doi.org/10.1017/S1461145711001829
  - Soomro, G. M., et al. (2008). Selective serotonin re-uptake inhibitors (SSRIs) versus placebo for obsessive compulsive disorder (OCD). *Cochrane Database of Systematic Reviews*, (1), CD001765. https://doi.org/10.1002/14651858.CD001765.pub3

#### 增效:小剂量抗精神病药
- 利培酮、阿立哌唑
- 用于 SSRI 单药疗效不佳者
- **参考**:Dold, M., et al. (2015). Antipsychotic augmentation of serotonin reuptake inhibitors in treatment-resistant obsessive-compulsive disorder: A meta-analysis. *Journal of Clinical Psychiatry*, *76*(9), e1166-e1174. https://doi.org/10.4088/JCP.14r10058

### 2.4 物理治疗

#### TMS(经颅磁刺激)
- 辅助治疗
- **参考**:Berlim, M. T., et al. (2013). Repetitive transcranial magnetic stimulation (rTMS) for obsessive-compulsive disorder (OCD): An exploratory meta-analysis. *Journal of Psychiatric Research*, *47*(7), 999-1006. https://doi.org/10.1016/j.jpsychires.2013.03.022

#### DBS(深部脑刺激)
- 用于难治性 OCD
- 靶点:内囊、腹侧纹状体、丘脑底核
- **参考**:Hamani, C., et al. (2014). Deep brain stimulation for obsessive-compulsive disorder. *Neurobiology of Disease*, *62*, 331-340. https://doi.org/10.1016/j.nbd.2013.12.001

## 三、特殊人群

### 3.1 儿童青少年
- **首选**:ERP + 家庭参与 CBT
- **注意**:家长要避免成为"强迫协助者"
- **参考**:Piacentini, J., et al. (2011). Controlled comparison of family cognitive behavioral therapy and psychoeducation/relaxation training for child obsessive-compulsive disorder. *Journal of the American Academy of Child and Adolescent Psychiatry*, *50*(11), 1149-1161. https://doi.org/10.1016/j.jaac.2011.08.013

### 3.2 围产期
- 风险评估 + 多学科协作
- 药物风险:氟西汀 C 级

### 3.3 共病
- 抑郁/焦虑:综合治疗
- 抽动:SSRI + 抗精神病药

## 四、预后与病程

- **缓解率**:40-50%
- **慢性化风险**:严重发病、合并人格障碍、治疗依从性差
- **长期治疗**:多数患者需 1-2 年维持期
