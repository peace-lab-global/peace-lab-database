---# 海马体功能 | Brain Hippocampus Function

> **文档类型**:神经科学核心结构 | Neuroscience Core Structure
> **目标**:系统阐述海马体的解剖学位置、细胞构成、核心功能及其在记忆与情绪中的作用

## 一、解剖学位置

海马体(Hippocampus)位于大脑内侧颞叶,属于边缘系统(Limbic System)核心结构:
- 形状弯曲,类似海马(因解剖学家 Julius Caesar Arantius 命名)
- 长度约 4-5 cm(成人)
- 与内嗅皮层(Entorhinal Cortex)、杏仁核(Amygdala)、下丘脑(Hypothalamus)紧密连接

## 二、核心功能

### 2.1 情景记忆(Episodic Memory)巩固
- 将短时记忆转化为长时记忆
- **位置细胞**(Place Cells):O'Keefe & Dostrovsky 1971 年发现,编码空间位置
- 时间细胞(Time Cells):近期研究(MacDonald et al., 2011)发现编码时间序列
- **参考**:
  - O'Keefe, J., & Nadel, L. (1978). *The hippocampus as a cognitive map*. Oxford University Press. https://doi.org/10.1093/acprof:oso/9780199214206.001.0001
  - Squire, L. R. (1992). Memory and the hippocampus: A synthesis from findings with rats, monkeys, and humans. *Psychological Review*, *99*(2), 195-231. https://doi.org/10.1037/0033-295X.99.2.195
  - Eichenbaum, H. (2017). The role of the hippocampus in navigation is memory. *Journal of Neurophysiology*, *117*(5), 1785-1796. https://doi.org/10.1152/jn.00005.2017

### 2.2 空间导航(Spatial Navigation)
- 与内嗅皮层的位置细胞(Grid Cells)协同工作
- 整合视觉、听觉、本体感觉形成认知地图
- **参考**:Moser, E. I., Kropff, E., & Moser, M. B. (2008). Place cells, grid cells, and the brain's spatial representation system. *Annual Review of Neuroscience*, *31*, 69-89. https://doi.org/10.1146/annurev.neuro.31.061307.090723

### 2.3 情绪调节
- 与杏仁核协同,调节应激反应
- 参与 PTSD、抑郁、焦虑的病理机制
- **参考**:Bremner, J. D. (2006). Traumatic stress: Effects on the brain. *Dialogues in Clinical Neuroscience*, *8*(4), 445-461. https://doi.org/10.31887/DCNS.2006.8.4/jbremner

## 三、神经发生(Neurogenesis)

- 成年海马齿状回(DG)持续产生新神经元
- 每天约 700 个新生神经元(成人)
- 影响因素:运动(BDNF↑)、学习、压力(皮质醇↑抑制)
- **参考**:
  - Eriksson, P. S., et al. (1998). Neurogenesis in the adult human hippocampus. *Nature Medicine*, *4*(11), 1313-1317. https://doi.org/10.1038/3305
  - Sorrells, S. F., et al. (2018). Human hippocampal neurogenesis drops sharply in children to undetectable levels in adults. *Nature*, *555*(7696), 377-381. https://doi.org/10.1038/nature25975
  - Boldrini, M., et al. (2018). Human hippocampal neurogenesis persists throughout aging. *Cell Stem Cell*, *22*(4), 589-599. https://doi.org/10.1016/j.stem.2018.03.015
  - Toda, T., & Gage, F. H. (2018). Review: Adult neurogenesis contributes to hippocampal plasticity. *Cell and Tissue Research*, *373*(3), 693-713. https://doi.org/10.1007/s00441-017-2735-4
