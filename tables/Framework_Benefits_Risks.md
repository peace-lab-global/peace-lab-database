Open hearts build lives... *JPSP*. | [GGSC: Loving-Kindness](https://ggia.berkeley.edu/practice/loving_kindness_meditation) |

Immediate effect of Surya Namaskar... *Int J Yoga*. | [Yoga Journal: Sun Salutations](https://www.yogajournal.com/poses/types/sun-salutations) |
